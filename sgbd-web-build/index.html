Robo Shotgun Birthday - Proof of Concept

Blast the other robots before they blast you. To contact the developer email nick@lostonestudios.com or check out @lostonestudios on Instagram and Twitter. There is no music only SFX.

Controls

  • W A S D to Move
  • Mouse cursor to aim
  • Left click to shoot
  • Right click to reload
  • Space bar to warp in the
    direction you are moving
